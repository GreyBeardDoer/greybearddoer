Dosa - Best All Purpose Snack

Description

Typically found in the southern half of India, the Dosa or Dhosa is an all purpose snack. People eat Dosa as breakfast, luch and even in dinner too. It is often enjoyed as an evening snack also.

The main reasons behind the popularity of this dish are - low calory, easy to cook, easy to digest.

Ingradients

  • Fermented rice paste
  • Mashed potatoes
  • Onions
  • Carrots
  • Beans
  • Green chillies
  • Salt
  • Refined oil

Steps

  1. Step 1: Heat a flat bottomed pan. Sprinkle some water on the hot surface.
  2. Step 2: Pour a cup of rice paste on the hot pan and spread it around in a big circle so that a thin film of rice is formed.
  3. Take a little bit of oil and sprinkle in all over the rice film. You can even use the bottom of a spoon to spread the oil evenly.
  4. Spread the onions, potatoes, fried vegetables on one half of the rice cake.
  5. Now roll up the cake over the veggies and serve.
