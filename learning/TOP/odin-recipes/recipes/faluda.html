Faluda - A Refreshinng Indian Dessert

Description

Faluda is a combination of ice-creams, fruits and sugar syrups. It is typically consumed during the scorching Indian summers. Along with Roohafza, it is also used as a refreshing iftaar drink during the holy Ramadan month.

Ingradients

  • Ice Cubes
  • Cold Water
  • Faluda Noodles
  • Ice Creams
  • Fruits
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Step 1: Boil the Faluda noodle untill they become soft and smooth.
